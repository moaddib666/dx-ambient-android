# Privacy Policy — DX Ambient

_Last updated: 2026-06-08_

DX Ambient ("the app") is an ambient-display app for Android TV, Google TV and
compatible Android devices. This policy explains what data the app does and does
not handle.

## Summary

**DX Ambient does not collect, store, or share any personal data.** There is no
account, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The core
experience works entirely offline.

## Data the app handles

- **Your media and scenes.** Videos, audio and images you import, and the scenes
  you create, are stored **only on your device** (and on any USB/local storage
  you choose). This data is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared by the app.
- **App settings.** Projector preferences (brightness, timers, performance mode,
  etc.) are stored locally on your device.
- **Device capability information.** The app reads playback capabilities of your
  device (e.g. supported codecs/resolution) to choose safe playback settings.
  This information stays on the device and is shown only to you.

## Permissions

- **Storage access (Storage Access Framework).** Used only to let you pick the
  media folders/files you want to play. The app accesses only what you select.
- **Internet / network state.** Used **only** by the optional YouTube mode.

## Optional YouTube mode

The app includes an optional, isolated YouTube mode that embeds the **official
YouTube IFrame player**. If — and only if — you use this mode, video is streamed
from Google's servers, and Google's own data practices apply to that playback.
See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and the YouTube
Terms of Service: https://www.youtube.com/t/terms. DX Ambient itself performs no
audio extraction, no downloading, and no background playback.

## Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal data and is suitable for general audiences. It
does not knowingly collect information from children.
