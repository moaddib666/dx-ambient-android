Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2026

DX Ambient does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The core experience works entirely offline.

DX Ambient (“the app”) is an ambient-display app for Android TV, Google TV, and compatible Android devices. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

Data the app handles

  • Your media and scenes. Videos, audio, and images you import, and the scenes you create, are stored only on your device (and on any USB or local storage you choose). This data is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared by the app.
  • App settings. Projector preferences (brightness, timers, performance mode, and similar) are stored locally on your device.
  • Device capability information. The app reads playback capabilities of your device (for example, supported codecs and resolution) to choose safe playback settings. This information stays on the device and is shown only to you.

Permissions

  • Storage access (Storage Access Framework). Used only to let you pick the media folders and files you want to play. The app accesses only what you select.
  • Internet / network state. Used only by the optional YouTube mode (see below).

Optional YouTube mode

The app includes an optional, isolated YouTube mode that embeds the official YouTube IFrame player. If — and only if — you use this mode, video is streamed from Google’s servers, and Google’s own data practices apply to that playback. See Google’s Privacy Policy and the YouTube Terms of Service. DX Ambient itself performs no audio extraction, no downloading, and no background playback.

Children’s privacy

The app does not collect personal data and is intended for a general adult audience. It does not knowingly collect information from children.

Data sharing and sale

We do not sell or share any user data with third parties, because the app does not collect any user data in the first place.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
