DX Ambient 1.8.0

• The screen stays awake during playback — no more lock mid-scene on phones and tablets
• Pausing now shows a designed overlay over an ambient backdrop instead of a black screen
• Touch gestures now mirror the remote 1:1: tap = play/pause, swipe sideways = switch scene, swipe up/down = change mask, Back = exit
• New scene type: Slideshow with cross-fade or Ken Burns drift
• New bundled scene: DX World — 110 Dimension-X vistas with the original score
